About

I believe the right conversation changes everything.

I'm Christine — a facilitator, connector, and strategist who has spent years helping creative teams find clarity, momentum, and each other.

My work lives at the intersection of ideas and action. I design and run workshops that help people think better together. I match clients with collaborators, campaigns, and creative partners that fit. And I write about all of it here.

I work with a small number of clients at a time — because the work deserves that kind of attention.
