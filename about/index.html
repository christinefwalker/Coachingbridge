About

Environment shapes behavior. The spaces we live, work, and create in influence performance, clarity, relationships, and identity.

I'm Christine — a connector, strategist, and ecosystem builder who helps people and ideas move forward through the right environments, relationships, and opportunities.

My work sits at the intersection of hospitality, real estate, community building, and creative entrepreneurship. I don't fit into one box — and that's the point. The ability to connect environment, behavior, emotional intelligence, and business strategy into one worldview is what makes the work land differently.

I help founders find clarity. I connect brands with aligned partners. I design experiences and spaces where meaningful things happen. And I write about all of it — the frameworks, the observations, the quiet patterns that shape how people build and grow.

Virginia Tech Dual degrees — Human Resource Management & Hospitality and Tourism
Real Estate Six-figure production, relocation-focused in St. Petersburg, FL
Sacred Space Studios Founder — creator infrastructure and community in Tampa Bay
Hospitality Background in hospitality operations, experience design, and service strategy
Wellness & Training Yoga teacher training, embodiment practices, and intentional living
Community Building Creator partnerships, mastermind facilitation, and ecosystem development

I believe the best work happens when people are in the right environment — physical, emotional, creative, relational, and professional. That belief runs through everything I build.

I work with a small number of people and projects at a time — because the work deserves that kind of attention. If you're building something that needs a connector, a strategist, or simply someone who sees the bigger picture — let's talk.
